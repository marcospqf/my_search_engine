            		<h1 class="title" id="page-title">Contact</h1>
                        <div class="block block-system block-main block-system-main odd block-without-title" id="block-system-main">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">

      <div class="view-content">
      <div class="item-list">      <h3>Company</h3>
    <ul>          <li class="views-row views-row-1 views-row-odd views-row-first faq-group">  
          <a href="#" title="Interested in working at Riot Games? " rel="faq-a-72" class="faq-question">Interested in working at Riot Games? </a>
<div id="faq-a-72" class="faq-answer"> <p>Visit our&nbsp;<a href="careers" target="_blank">Careers</a>&nbsp;section, which&nbsp;includes information about what it&#39;s like to be a Rioter, how to apply for roles and what sorts of opportunities are available. If you&#39;re looking for career advice,&nbsp;try hitting up our <a href="http://www.linkedin.com/groups/Inside-Riot-Games-People-Game-3544410?gid=3544410">LinkedIn group</a>. LinkedIn is a site for professionally focused discussion and some seekers have received good advice when they ask for help there. Please do try some internet searches first, though. There&#39;s lots of great advice already published online about how to break into the games industry, and there are a number of online resources that will put you on the right path. We hire self-starters, so that means doing some good ol&#39; fashioned Googling before you hit us up with questions.</p>
 </div>  </li>
          <li class="views-row views-row-2 views-row-even views-row-last faq-group">  
          <a href="#" title="Looking for a tour of the Riot offices? " rel="faq-a-496" class="faq-question">Looking for a tour of the Riot offices? </a>
<div id="faq-a-496" class="faq-answer"> <p>We now offer sneak peeks into our Los Angeles, California headquarters. To learn more, click <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/tours" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p>Can&#39;t make it down to LA, but still want a look? Check out this behind-the-scenes look of our&nbsp;<a href="http://www.riotgames.com/articles/20150420/1663/twas-night-our-move-la" target="_blank">new office</a>!</p>
 </div>  </li>
        <!-- <li class="ftr-link"></li> -->
  </ul></div><div class="item-list">      <h3>Press</h3>
    <ul>          <li class="views-row views-row-1 views-row-odd views-row-first views-row-last faq-group">  
          <a href="#" title="In need of press materials? Have a media request? " rel="faq-a-73" class="faq-question">In need of press materials? Have a media request? </a>
<div id="faq-a-73" class="faq-answer"> <p>If you&#39;re with the <strong>media or press </strong>and want to talk, shoot a note to&nbsp;<a href="mailto:media@riotgames.com">the folks in Riot Communications</a>.&nbsp;(If you&#39;re not media or press and you send a note there, we&#39;ll&nbsp;<a href="http://youtu.be/gAYL5H46QnQ" target="_blank">throw it on the ground</a>. Media only, man.)&nbsp;</p>
 </div>  </li>
        <!-- <li class="ftr-link"></li> -->
  </ul></div><div class="item-list">      <h3>League of Legends</h3>
    <ul>          <li class="views-row views-row-1 views-row-odd views-row-first faq-group">  
          <a href="#" title="Can I use your IP?" rel="faq-a-2357" class="faq-question">Can I use your IP?</a>
<div id="faq-a-2357" class="faq-answer"> <p>Want to create an app, website, t-shirt or something else with our intellectual property, but don&rsquo;t know what&rsquo;s legally kosher? &nbsp;Check out our <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/legal-jibber-jabber">Legal Jibber Jabber</a>. If you&rsquo;re trying to get feedback on an app or website, register it at our <a href="https://developer.riotgames.com/">Developer Portal</a>.</p>
 </div>  </li>
          <li class="views-row views-row-2 views-row-even faq-group">  
          <a href="#" title="Got an awesome idea for League of Legends? " rel="faq-a-495" class="faq-question">Got an awesome idea for League of Legends? </a>
<div id="faq-a-495" class="faq-answer"> <p>If you&#39;ve got an awesome idea for a skin, champion or how to improve <em>League of Legends</em>, the best place for these is <a href="http://boards.na.leagueoflegends.com/" target="_blank">http://boards.na.leagueoflegends.com/</a>.&nbsp;We do read the forums and pay attention to the ideas that players share with us there.&nbsp;</p>
<p>For a bunch of reasons, we can&#39;t accept submissions for skin or champion concepts, but we&#39;ve noticed that many summoners love coming up with concepts so we created <a href="http://boards.na.leagueoflegends.com/en/c/skin-champion-concepts" target="_blank"><span class="s1">this forum</span></a> where you can post and share these ideas. If you post here, you&#39;ll get others&#39; feedback on your concept.</p>
<p>Please note that this forum&#39;s purpose isn&#39;t to help Riot find the newest champion of the League. Rather, we want to give you a platform to express your creativity.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
 </div>  </li>
          <li class="views-row views-row-3 views-row-odd faq-group">  
          <a href="#" title="Looking to start a tournament?" rel="faq-a-2358" class="faq-question">Looking to start a tournament?</a>
<div id="faq-a-2358" class="faq-answer"> If you&#39;re setting up a tournament and looking for support, check out our <a href="http://na.leagueoflegends.com/en/community/prized-events" target="_blank">Prized Events page</a> where you&rsquo;ll be able to submit an application. If you can&rsquo;t find your answer there, hit up <a href="mailto:tournaments@riotgames.com">tournaments@riotgames.com</a>.
 </div>  </li>
          <li class="views-row views-row-4 views-row-even views-row-last faq-group">  
          <a href="#" title="Need help putting League of Legends videos on the YouTubes?" rel="faq-a-493" class="faq-question">Need help putting League of Legends videos on the YouTubes?</a>
<div id="faq-a-493" class="faq-answer"> <p>If you&#39;d like to put your&nbsp;<em>League of Legends</em>&nbsp;fan videos on YouTube or another streaming video distributor, but need to cut through the proverbial red tape, check out the official policy on advertising in your&nbsp;<em>League of Legends</em>&nbsp;videos&nbsp;<a href="/legal-jibber-jabber" target="_blank">here</a>.&nbsp;</p>
 </div>  </li>
        <!-- <li class="ftr-link"></li> -->
  </ul></div><div class="item-list">      <h3>Player Support &amp; Billing</h3>
    <ul>          <li class="views-row views-row-1 views-row-odd views-row-first views-row-last faq-group">  
          <a href="#" title="Have a billing issue or experiencing a problem with League of Legends?" rel="faq-a-75" class="faq-question">Have a billing issue or experiencing a problem with League of Legends?</a>
<div id="faq-a-75" class="faq-answer"> <p>Please submit a ticket at <a href="http://support.leagueoflegends.com" target="_blank">support.leagueoflegends.com</a>. This is the <strong>fastest</strong> way to get your issue resolved. We apologize for your difficulties and want to make things right. Taking your <em>League of Legends</em> support issues directly to the <em>League of Legends</em> support team is the best way to get speedy resolution.&nbsp;Support questions or tickets submitted to <em>League of Legends</em> or Riot Games email addresses will end up being re-directed back to the <a href="http://support.leagueoflegends.com" target="_blank"><em>League of Legends</em> support site.</a></p>
 </div>  </li>
        <!-- <li class="ftr-link"></li> -->
  </ul></div><div class="item-list">      <h3>Misc</h3>
