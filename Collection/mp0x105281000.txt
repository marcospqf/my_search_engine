    <span class="print-link"></span><div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"> <p>Welcome to Riot Tours!</p>
<p>When we made the move to our <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/articles/20150423/1660/%E2%80%98twas-night-our-move-la" target="_blank">new headquarters in West Los Angeles</a>, we received lots of requests to visit the space. We took some time to settle in and spiff up the joint, and now we&rsquo;re excited to welcome players to our digs. The tour explores Riot culture, and includes a look at how League and the community influenced the design of our campus.</p>
<p>We launched player tours into beta mode in February and it&rsquo;s been a great experience so far! While we&rsquo;re getting our sea legs, there&rsquo;ll be a limited number of dates available, so keep an eye out for more information.</p>
<p>To sign up for a tour, just select the date you&rsquo;re interested in and answer a few questions. Once you submit your request, you&rsquo;ll receive a registration confirmation within three business days. If tours are full, or available dates don&rsquo;t fit into your schedule, don&rsquo;t worry! We&rsquo;ll release additional dates during the first week of each month, so check back often.</p>
<p>For further information about tours, check out the <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/tours/faqs" target="_blank">Tours FAQ</a>.</p>

            		<h1 class="title" id="page-title">Security Vulnerability Reporting</h1>
                        <div class="block block-system block-main block-system-main odd block-without-title" id="block-system-main">
  <div class="block-inner clearfix">
                
    <div class="content clearfix">
      <article about="/security-vulnerability-reporting" typeof="sioc:Item foaf:Document" class="node node-page node-published node-not-promoted node-not-sticky author-sdowney odd clearfix" id="node-page-1210">
        <span property="dc:title" content="Security Vulnerability Reporting" class="rdf-meta element-hidden"></span><span property="sioc:num_replies" content="0" datatype="xsd:integer" class="rdf-meta element-hidden"></span>    
  
  <div class="content clearfix">
    <span class="print-link"></span><div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"> <p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Players and the security research community help us quickly repair security problems by reporting vulnerabilities and putting them on our radar.</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">To report a security issue, just shoot us an email at <a href="mailto:SOC@riotgames.com">SOC@riotgames.com</a> (PGP key available at the bottom of this page).</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">We welcome reports of all security vulnerabilities, including:</span></p>
<ul>
	<li>
		Web security problems (e.g. cross-site scripting and SQL injection problems)</li>
	<li>
		Game exploits (e.g. insta-win bugs or third party game modifications)</li>
	<li>
		Other security concerns (e.g. infrastructure security problems, information disclosure issues)</li>
</ul>
<p>We are also currently running a <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/articles/20141121/1622/hunt-introducing-riot%E2%80%99s-bug-bounty-program" target="_blank">closed-beta bounty program</a> hosted on HackerOne to reward security researchers.&nbsp;Researchers who successfully identify and report particularly severe security issues will receive an appropriate bounty and an invite to access this program.</p>
<h4 dir="ltr">
	<span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Pro-Tips for Scoring A Bounty</span></h4>
<p dir="ltr"><strong><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Reports that are more likely to qualify for a bounty have:</span></strong></p>
<ul dir="ltr">
	<li>
		<span>Easy-to-follow reproduction steps</span></li>
	<li>
		Bug descriptions that specify the scope of the vulnerability</li>
	<li>
		Clear details about how the vulnerability can be directly leveraged as part of an exploit against players or Riot</li>
	<li>
		Examples of bug types that commonly qualify for a bounty include XSS, CSRF, SQL injection, authorization issues, gameplay exploits and the like</li>
</ul>
<p dir="ltr"><strong><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Reports that are less likely to qualify for a bounty (unless you found a cool way to exploit one of these):</span></strong></p>
<ul>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Leverage automated scanners without a narrow scoping or throttled request rate that creates disruptive results like degraded service or publicly visible spam on our community discussion boards/forums</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Missing HTTP Headers such as X-Content-Type, X-Frame-Options and X-XSS-Protection</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Presence of banner or version information in HTTP responses</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Logout CSRF vulnerabilities</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Expected behavior which a researcher might deem to be insecure such as reCAPTCHA accepting an incorrect word</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Vulnerabilities in third party applications which make use of Riot Games APIs</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Flaws specific to out-of-date browsers/plugins</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Lack of the Secure and HTTPOnly flags on non-sensitive cookies</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Clickjacking on pages without authentication or sensitive state changes</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Error messages void of sensitive data</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4">Self-XSS scenarios that would require additional user interaction, including the user manually inputting the XSS payload</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-78626139-139b-c8ab-051b-67c3e8674fb4"><em>League of Legends</em> gameplay bugs</span></p>
	</li>
</ul>
<p>For other issues with your account, head over to the <a href="https://support.leagueoflegends.com/home" target="_blank">Player Support</a> page.</p>
