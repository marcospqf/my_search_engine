We&#39;re always looking for talented and driven people to </span><a href="http://www.riotgames.com/careers">join us</a> <span class="print-footnote">[1]</span> as we strive to create player-focused games.</p>
<p><span id="docs-internal-guid-2a1b6b8d-eeac-1da0-299a-3c4b54b104ca">Riot&#39;s a global company &ndash; in addition to the Los Angeles headquarters (located just minutes from the beach), we&#39;ve got outposts in Berlin, Dublin, Hong Kong, Istanbul, Mexico City, Moscow, New York, Paris, Santiago, Sao Paulo, Seoul, San Francisco Bay Area, Shanghai, Singapore, St. Louis, Sydney, Taipei, and Tokyo.&nbsp;</span></p>
<h2>
	RIOT CULTURE</h2>
<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-2a1b6b8d-eeac-9a34-142e-659aed3afa5c">Our goal is to become the most player-focused company in the world. With this mission in mind, we develop high-quality games and services enjoyed by </span><a href="http://www.riotgames.com/articles/20140711/1322/league-players-reach-new-heights-2014">tens of millions</a> <span class="print-footnote">[2]</span> of players around the world.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-2a1b6b8d-eeac-9a34-142e-659aed3afa5c">We also take play seriously. For us, it&rsquo;s never &quot;just a game.&quot; We play lots of games, especially our own. Everyone&rsquo;s first day at Riot begins with a playtest. We try to make it a daily habit. </span><a href="http://i.imgur.com/qMTJwGK.gif">Although we&rsquo;re serious professionals</a> <span class="print-footnote">[3]</span>, we don&rsquo;t take ourselves too seriously. Whether it&rsquo;s memes in presentations or the PC Bangs built into the heart of new offices, we make time for play and fun.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-2a1b6b8d-eeac-9a34-142e-659aed3afa5c">At our core, we&#39;re a group of passionate people who embrace new challenges, thrive in finding smart, out-of-the-box solutions, </span>and love <em>League of Legends</em> and this industry. If this sounds like a team you&#39;d like to be a part of, check out <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/careers">our job openings</a> <span class="print-footnote">[1]</span> across all of our offices.</p>
<h2>
	PERKS OF BEING A RIOTER</h2>
<p>As we focus on creating the best player experiences, we also strive to create a place where Rioters will thrive.&nbsp;Here are a few of the benefits for Rioters in <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/articles/20110615/233/riot-games-santa-monica" target="_blank">L</a> <span class="print-footnote">[4]</span><a href="http://www.riotgames.com/articles/20150501/233/riot-games-los-angeles" target="_blank">os Angeles</a> <span class="print-footnote">[5]</span>:</p>
<h3>
	THE BASICS</h3>
<ul>
	<li>
		Highly competitive salary</li>
	<li>
		Choice between four subsidized HMO and PPO medical insurance plans</li>
	<li>
		Subsidized vision insurance plan</li>
	<li>
		PPO dental insurance plan</li>
	<li>
		Benefits coverage for domestic partners</li>
	<li>
		Matching 401k</li>
	<li>
		Short term and long term disability insurance</li>
	<li>
		Life insurance (free basic life insurance, plus voluntary life insurance)</li>
</ul>
<h3>
	PERKS BEYOND THE NORM</h3>
<ul>
	<li>
		Open PTO (Paid Time Off)</li>
	<li>
		Flexible hours</li>
	<li>
		Rioter donation matching</li>
	<li>
		On-site, full service cafeteria offering fully subsidized meals and <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/sites/default/files/Bilgewater.jpg" target="_blank">coffee bar</a> <span class="print-footnote">[6]</span></li>
	<li>
		Office kitchens stocked with free cereal, fresh fruit, snacks, tea, and fresh ground coffee</li>
	<li>
		All-company &quot;Taste the Riot&quot; fairs and <a href="http://thunderdome.riotgames.com/" target="_blank">&quot;Thunderdome&quot; hackathons</a> <span class="print-footnote">[7]</span> (infused with adult beverages)</li>
	<li>
		Ongoing education and training through Riot U</li>

</ul>
<ul>
