    <div class="field field-name-field-main-image field-type-image field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><img typeof="foaf:Image" src="http://www.riotgames.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_image/public/content/article/images/main/NewRG-Header1.png?itok=WCpujRQR" width="700" height="300" alt="New Riotgames.com " /></div></div></div><div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"> <p>We tried to make it better than the old RiotGames.com, which is kind of a tall order since the old site cost us <em>several thousand dollars</em> to make, had sweet animated GIFs, occasionally used ALL CAPS and was generally a workhorse of a site that we used for three frickin&#39; years.</p>
<p>It was time for a new site. This new site&#39;s designed to do a few things better:</p>
<ul>
	<li>
		Introduce <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/about" target="_blank">the Riot Manifesto</a>, a description of why we do what we do</li>
	<li>
		Post brief explanations of stuff like <a href="/articles/20121116/245/honor-helps-sanitize-bad-behavior" target="_blank">Honor</a> for peeps who might not frequent the forums</li>
	<li>
		Leak the occasional email (<a href="/articles/20121004/567/corporate-veigar-reluctantly-spreads-holiday-cheer" target="_blank">true story</a>)</li>
	<li>
		Share a little taste of <a href="/articles/20070101/547/working-riot" target="_blank">what it&#39;s like to work at Riot</a></li>
	<li>
		Make it easy to search and sort through our <a href="/careers" target="_blank">megalisting of open roles</a></li>
	<li>
		Humblebrag about how tough it can be to find a trophy case that fits <a href="/articles/20091209/546/awards" target="_blank">all the nice things</a> some people say about Riot</li>
	<li>
		Use your full browser resolution &ndash; because screen real estate is a horrible thing to waste</li>
</ul>
<p>And now it&#39;s time to hear your thoughts. We know some stuff about this new site is janky right now. That&#39;s not by design, it&#39;s just the messy, wrinkly look that new sites have shortly after birth. We&#39;re trying to clean this baby up, but would appreciate any and all feedback. Spot a defect? Have an idea for what we could do to improve what you see here? Lob an email over to <a href="mailto:info@riotgames.com">info@riotgames.com</a> and we&#39;ll jump on it. &nbsp;</p>
