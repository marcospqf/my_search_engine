  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d703-e822-703d-f3fbc596ba09">To qualify for an internship, you&#39;ve gotta be a full-time student in an accredited undergrad, Masters, MBA, PhD, or JD program and remain a full-time student the semester after the internship. We give heavy preference to students looking for internships for the summer before their final year of schooling. Those who&#39;ve graduated already or are graduating soon should check out our full-time openings on the <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/careers" target="_blank">Careers page.</a></span></p>

  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d707-c8f4-c73d-2b53e4867a88">Interns must be old enough to legally work where the Riot office is located.   <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d70b-5e5b-4106-539e8133c70a">All of them. We&rsquo;ve got people in marketing who come from engineering backgrounds, and people in engineering who come from liberal arts backgrounds. That said, some teams may require specific skills. Where possible, we&rsquo;ve spelled out those requirements in the job description. If you&rsquo;re thinking a few years ahead, check out the</span><a href="http://www.riotgames.com/careers" target="_blank"> Careers</a> page for info on the specific skills that we value in full-time Rioters.</p>

  <div class="views-row views-row-16 views-row-even">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1477"><h4>Could an internship turn into an actual job?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-a7e6465b-8550-2633-ccec-537b80f7be3b">Yes, one of the reasons we have an internship program is to identify (and hire) top talent who&rsquo;ll be graduating soon.</span></p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-17 views-row-odd">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1478"><h4>I have a few more questions, where can I learn more?  </h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-a7e6465b-8551-1d56-e2ca-bbf3b6db4e72">Venture over to our</span><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/groups/3544410/3544410-6179696359273160707" target="_blank"> LinkedIn internship thread</a>&nbsp;and we&#39;ll try to answer your questions there.</p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-18 views-row-even">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1479"><h4>Is there any way to apply for a team that doesn’t have a position listed?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-a7e6465b-8553-0a0e-1d3d-d757bbc11de6">Sorry, we&#39;ve only got spots open for those teams that are listed.&nbsp;</span></p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-19 views-row-odd">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1436"><h4>Why are the internship job descriptions and requirements so ambiguous?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p>We&#39;re only guessing what an intern might do nine months from now &ndash; Riot moves pretty quickly. We&#39;re looking for broadly-capable interns who can handle whatever comes their way next summer.</p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-20 views-row-even">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1437"><h4>I don&#039;t meet all the requirements, should I still apply?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d71d-7d64-a9fb-9d8671825713">Maybe. Each team crafts their own job descriptions, so most of the requirements listed are pretty important, but what you&#39;re missing might not be essential. Or the person who&#39;s hiring could give you a thumbs-up if you bring spades of experience or skill in something else.&nbsp;</span></p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-21 views-row-odd">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1482"><h4>How can I make my application stand out?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d71e-5d52-f6b8-76eb7e9eda5b">There are lots of ways to stand out! We&#39;re looking for smart, talented students who carve their own tracks. The best way to show us you have what it takes is to accomplish something extraordinary: create a game or a mod, or craft a strong portfolio that demonstrates your aptitude. If you can get a &ldquo;WOW!&rdquo; or a fist-bump from your buddies, then chances are it&rsquo;s pretty incredible.</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d71e-5d52-f6b8-76eb7e9eda5b">Here are just a few examples of stuff former Riot NA interns did prior to applying:</span></p>
<ul>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d71e-5d52-f6b8-76eb7e9eda5b">Created high-value content on a well-known blog (or created a well-known blog)</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d71e-5d52-f6b8-76eb7e9eda5b">Started a software development company and shipped a product</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d71e-5d52-f6b8-76eb7e9eda5b">Created artwork that blew minds and won competitions</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d71e-5d52-f6b8-76eb7e9eda5b">Demonstrated mastery of critical technologies we use (e.g., Hadoop, Drupal, Scaleform and Adobe Air) by shipping products using those technologies</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d71e-5d52-f6b8-76eb7e9eda5b">Led esports teams to victory in major </span><em>League of Legends</em> tournaments</p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
		<span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d71e-5d52-f6b8-76eb7e9eda5b">Self-published a novel or three&nbsp;</span></li>
</ul>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-22 views-row-even">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1483"><h4>I know how to do a lot of things, which position should I apply for?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d71f-5a88-fe63-8c3df83632db">We love awesome and talented people who&rsquo;re good at lots of things! Do some digging to figure out where your skills intersect with your passions and see which internship fits you best. Don&rsquo;t apply for all of them! If you&rsquo;ve got a burning passion for a few teams, then give each one a shot. Come up with specific, compelling pitches for each, and let us know why you&rsquo;re the perfect fit for them. Keep in mind that applying for more positions won&rsquo;t increase your chances and spamming could hurt you.</span></p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-23 views-row-odd">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1484"><h4>To whom should I address my cover letter?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-a7e6465b-855f-cf52-ac29-6fdc30965c15">&ldquo;Dear Riot&rdquo; or &ldquo;Dear Rioters&rdquo; works!</span></p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-24 views-row-even">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1485"><h4>How long should my cover letter and resume be?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d720-4d2b-a6a8-608a68652508">We don&rsquo;t have a hard cutoff but check with your school&rsquo;s career services office for generally accepted practices.</span></p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-25 views-row-odd">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1486"><h4>Can I submit extra materials?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-a7e6465b-8580-e228-3501-41dfe8dd460a">Sure! Most positions require only a cover letter and resume, so anything extra is up to you as long as you can submit it electronically with your application. Use your best judgment; a link to a 10-minute monologue video is probably too much.</span></p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-26 views-row-even">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1487"><h4> Do I have to write my cover letter? Or can it be in whatever form I dream of?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d721-8640-23a4-2445017d746e">Feel free to be creative and flex your odd and extraordinary skills. As long as you can submit it via the app portal or we can access it online, you&rsquo;re good to go. Just keep in mind that a well-executed application is always better than a mediocre creative experiment.</span></p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-27 views-row-odd">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1488"><h4>Should I include letters of recommendation or references?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-a7e6465b-8582-9340-222e-e195dfd5fc85">They won&rsquo;t come into play until later in the interview process, so stash them somewhere safe for now.</span></p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-28 views-row-even">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1489"><h4>Can you give me feedback on my application materials?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d722-ba2f-3bdf-83116007c69d">Unfortunately, we can&rsquo;t. The volume of applications is such that we&rsquo;d need a dedicated feedback team to provide comments on every application. There are resources and people in our</span><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/groups/3544410/3544410-6179696359273160707" target="_blank"> LinkedIn community</a> who may be willing to give feedback, though.</p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-29 views-row-odd">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1490"><h4>Should I send in a hard copy of my application?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d723-f0a7-9f24-f52dd8d31dd9">Save a sapling and stick to using our website when submitting your materials.&nbsp;</span></p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-30 views-row-even">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1491"><h4>I’m having technical issues with the application portal, who can help me?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-a7e6465b-8584-ad5d-1e42-b201c1c1d0b6">Technology is funky sometimes. Head over to our</span><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/groups/3544410/3544410-6179696359273160707" target="_blank"> LinkedIn internship thread</a>&nbsp;and post your question. Someone there can help! &nbsp;</p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-31 views-row-odd">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1439"><h4>BAM! I just applied, now what?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d72c-7e2a-5969-ec2efa285c69">If your application collects sufficient kudos, we&#39;ll be in touch via email to set up some phone convos, so keep an eye on your inbox and spam folder. During these calls, we&#39;ll ask about your interests, expertise, what you want to learn from us and what we can learn from you. If the calls go well, we&#39;ll invite you to Riot for a final round of interviews and to meet the team. Riot covers flights, accommodations, food, and transportation for the interviews.</span></p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-32 views-row-even">
      
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-question">        <div class="field-content"><a id="question-1440"><h4>Can I update my application with new stuff or correct an error?</h4></a></div>  </div>  
  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d72d-0cd9-c082-4ef68e66aa53">There will be no way to update your application once it has been submitted. You will need to resubmit a brand new application--so it is definitely worth double checking before hitting the submit button.</span></p>
 </div>  </div>  </div>
  <div class="views-row views-row-33 views-row-odd">

  <div class="views-field views-field-field-answer">        <div class="field-content"> <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-229a2191-d72f-1458-0840-225893d4151b">Unfortunately, no. We promise to respond to every applicant via email by the response dates listed in the FAQ and in the job descriptions.</span></p>
