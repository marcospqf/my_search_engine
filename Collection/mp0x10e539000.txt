<p>When we made the move to our <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/articles/20150423/1660/%E2%80%98twas-night-our-move-la" target="_blank">new headquarters in West Los Angeles</a>, we received lots of requests to visit the space. <p>To sign up for a tour, just select the date you&rsquo;re interested in and answer a few questions. Once you submit your request, you&rsquo;ll receive a registration confirmation within three business days. If tours are full, or available dates don&rsquo;t fit into your schedule, don&rsquo;t worry! We&rsquo;ll release additional dates during the first week of each month, so check back often.</p>
<p>For further information about tours, check out the <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/tours/faqs" target="_blank">Tours FAQ</a>.</p>
