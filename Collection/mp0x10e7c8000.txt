    <span class="print-link"></span><div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"> <p>Welcome to Riot Tours!</p>
<p>When we made the move to our <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/articles/20150423/1660/%E2%80%98twas-night-our-move-la" target="_blank">new headquarters in West Los Angeles</a>, we received lots of requests to visit the space. We took some time to settle in and spiff up the joint, and now we&rsquo;re excited to welcome players to our digs. The tour explores Riot culture, and includes a look at how League and the community influenced the design of our campus.</p>
<p>We launched player tours into beta mode in February and it&rsquo;s been a great experience so far! While we&rsquo;re getting our sea legs, there&rsquo;ll be a limited number of dates available, so keep an eye out for more information.</p>
<p>To sign up for a tour, just select the date you&rsquo;re interested in and answer a few questions. Once you submit your request, you&rsquo;ll receive a registration confirmation within three business days. If tours are full, or available dates don&rsquo;t fit into your schedule, don&rsquo;t worry! We&rsquo;ll release additional dates during the first week of each month, so check back often.</p>
<p>For further information about tours, check out the <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/tours/faqs" target="_blank">Tours FAQ</a>.</p>

    <span class="print-link"></span><div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"> <h2>
	CREAREA DE MATERIALE VIDEO RIOT GAMES &amp; POLITICA DE UTILIZARE</h2>
<p>Coninutul creat de comunitatea noastr inspir, informeaz i distreaz deopotriv angajaii Riot i juctorii. Sperm s vedem c&acirc;t mai multe materiale video care utilizeaz coninut Riot Games i League of Legends. Acest ghid util v va ajuta s v asigurai c orice material video care folosete elemente ce face parte din proprietatea noastr intelectual (cum ar fi imagini video, muzic, sunete i alte tipuri de coninut) este legal &icirc;nainte de a fi postat pe YouTube, transmis pe site-uri de tipul own3D sau conceput doar pentru voi i prietenii votri. Aceast politic ar trebui s v permit s primii orice tip de acord pentru materialul video de care avei nevoie de pe site-uri precum YouTube i s stabileasc mai clar tipurile de materiale video pe care le agrem.</p>
<ul>
	<li>
		<strong>Non-profit (dac nu sunt denumite diferit &icirc;n aceast Politic!). Toate materialele video trebuie puse la dispoziia publicului fr a se solicita taxe de acces vizitatorilor sau fr a impune alte restricii. &Icirc;n general, aceasta &icirc;nseamn:</strong>
		<ul>
			<li>
				Nu putei cere nicio tax, sondaj, descrcare (&icirc;n afar de cea a materialului video &icirc;n sine) sau orice alt aciune &icirc;n schimbul accesului la materialul video.</li>
			<li>
				Putei publica un material video pe un site care are o opiune de plat &quot;Premium&quot; cu condiia ca acel material video s fie disponibil i pentru utilizatorii care nu pltesc.</li>
			<li>
				Putei afia reclame sau alte surse de venit care nu &icirc;mpiedic accesul utilizatorului la materialul video, &icirc;n condiiile ghidului &quot;Fr licen&quot; de mai jos.</li>
		</ul>
	</li>
</ul>
<ul>
	<li>
		<strong>Fr licen. &Icirc;n general, nu putei oferi licena pentru materialele voastre video niciunui ter &icirc;n schimbul unei anumite taxe sau oricrei alte valori fr acordul nostru prealabil. Exist &icirc;ns aceste excepii importante:</strong>
		<ul>
			<li>
				Programe partenere ale YouTube sau urmtoarele site-uri de streaming: own3D.tv, Ustream.tv, Twitch.tv, Justin.tv, Blip.tv (nu este necesar permisiunea noastr prealabil).</li>
			<li>
				Pentru orice alt form de oferire a licenei din partea voastr (cum ar fi festivaluri de film, concursuri sau transmisiuni ale terilor), este necesar permisiunea noastr expres scris.&nbsp;</li>
			<li>
				Trimitei toate solicitrile la&nbsp;<a href="mailto:fansites@riotgames.com">fansites@riotgames.com</a>.</li>
		</ul>
	</li>
</ul>
<ul>
	<li>
		<strong>Sponsorizrile sunt acceptate. Dac vrei s afiai sigla sponsorului la &icirc;nceputul materialului, la credite sau vrei s &icirc;i anunai, foarte bine. Avem doar c&acirc;teva reguli simple:</strong>
		<ul>
			<li>
				Meninei orice menionare a sponsorului la o lungime minim, rezonabil.</li>
			<li>
				S fie clar (fie c menionai un sponsor sau utilizai meniunea &quot;sponsorizat de&quot; alturi de sigla lor) faptul c&nbsp; acetia au doar rolul de sponsori.</li>
			<li>
				Nu folosii siglele Riot Games (sau League of Legends) fr a avea permisiunea noastr expres.&nbsp;</li>
		</ul>
	</li>
</ul>
<ul>
	<li>
		<strong>Avei grij s fie totul (relativ) decent. Nu se accept absolut niciun coninut cu mesaje rasiste, sexiste, homofobe sau alte tipuri de coninut ofensator. V cerem, de asemenea, ca materialele voastre video s fie centrate asupra jocurilor i s pstreze distana fa de polemici fi religioase, politice sau alte tipuri de discuii controversate.</strong></li>
</ul>
<ul>
	<li>
		<strong>Respectai ali creatori. &Icirc;n afar de coninutul aflat sub proprietatea intelectual a Riot, nu includei altceva &icirc;n afar de creaiile voastre originale dec&acirc;t &icirc;n cazul &icirc;n care avei permisiunea creatorului acestor materiale. Nu dai bani la avocai!</strong></li>
</ul>
<p>Dac avei &icirc;ntrebri &icirc;n legtur cu aceast politic, contactai-ne la&nbsp;<a href="mailto:fansites@riotgames.com">fansites@riotgames.com</a>.</p>

    <div class="field field-name-field-main-image field-type-image field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><img typeof="foaf:Image" src="http://www.riotgames.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_image/public/content/article/images/main/map-of-internet.png?itok=73eRTB0s" width="700" height="300" alt="" /></div></div></div><div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"> <p>We&rsquo;ve <a href="/peering-policy" target="_blank">just published</a> some info about how we peer. It&rsquo;s some super technical stuff that is probably most interesting to internet service providers (ISPs) interested in peering with Riot. So why are we posting it at all?</p>
<p>We want to peer with more ISPs because when we peer, we&rsquo;re able to exchange traffic without a lot of extra hops, skips and jumps around the interwebs. Peering helps us create the shortest possible path across the internet to players by directly connecting to the internet service providers. With this relationship, we&rsquo;re not only able to directly connect, but we can also help those internet service providers address specific problems that may be affecting their service or <em>League of Legends</em> play. Peering also adds additional diversity of connections, which helps us defend when DDoSers attack.</p>
<p>Bottom line: peering helps us help ISPs, which helps players, so we&rsquo;re trying to do more of it. With it, there&rsquo;s a more direct connection between players and <em>League of Legends</em> and that means better pings for players and better connectivity for Riot&rsquo;s game services. <a href="http://gifrific.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/anchorman-jump-in-air.gif" target="_blank">Everyone&rsquo;s a winner</a>!</p>
