<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-2a1b6b8d-eeac-9a34-142e-659aed3afa5c">At our core, we&#39;re a group of passionate people who embrace new challenges, thrive in finding smart, out-of-the-box solutions, </span>and love <em>League of Legends</em> and this industry.
