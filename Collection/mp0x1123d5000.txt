  <div class="content clearfix">
    <div class="field field-name-field-main-image field-type-image field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><img typeof="foaf:Image" src="http://www.riotgames.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_image/public/content/article/images/main/Pax_main.png?itok=I2d4ceBD" width="700" height="300" alt="" /></div></div></div><div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"> <p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-026749fd-499b-3551-1e1e-e209c42ccee4">Like a Lovecraftian leviathan rising from Boston harbor, PAX East -- Penny Arcade&rsquo;s gaming and geek culture convention, running from April 11 - 13 -- is nearly upon us. We&rsquo;re holding down trusty booth #424 over at the Boston Convention and Exhibition Center, so swing by and join us for more community events than Cthulhu has tentacles on his gross face.</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-026749fd-499b-3551-1e1e-e209c42ccee4">These community events include no-holds-barred </span><em>League</em> matches between players and Rioters, live art demos, cosplay showcases, design Q&amp;A sessions, an in-depth presentation on the making of the<a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0nlJuwO0GDs" target="_blank"> &ldquo;Get Jinxed&rdquo;</a> music video, and some very special <em>League</em> community guest appearances.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-026749fd-499b-3551-1e1e-e209c42ccee4">We&rsquo;re also hosting panels all con long where we hope to connect with players and start conversations about how we approach game design, the </span><em>League</em> community, and our champion relaunch process. To get a full look at Riot&rsquo;s PAX East panels, cast your gaze below:</p>
<ul dir="ltr">
	<li>
		<span id="docs-internal-guid-026749fd-493a-ddd3-788d-b19d0df9198c"><a href="http://east.paxsite.com/schedule/panel/connecting-and-supporting-the-league-of-legends-community" target="_blank">Connecting and Supporting the<em> League of Legends </em>Community</a></span> &ndash; Friday, April 11 from 2:30 pm &ndash; 3:30 pm, Dragonfly Theater</li>
</ul>
<p class="rteindent1"><span id="docs-internal-guid-026749fd-493a-ddd3-788d-b19d0df9198c">At Riot Games, no day goes by that we&#39;re not blown away by actions and interactions of the </span><em>League of Legends </em>community. From collegiate clubs to cosplayers, from streamers to artists, from fan sites to tournament organizers, the community&#39;s taken the stage to expand and redefine the <em>League of Legends</em> experience for everyone. In this panel, we&rsquo;ll discuss how we challenge the conventions of traditional PR and Community disciplines to build a different kind of relationship with players.</p>
<ul dir="ltr">
	<li>
		<span id="docs-internal-guid-026749fd-493a-ddd3-788d-b19d0df9198c"><a href="http://east.paxsite.com/schedule/panel/the-art-of-relaunching-champions-in-league-of-legends" target="_blank">The Art of Relaunching Champions in <em>League of Legends</em></a></span> &ndash; Saturday, April 12 from 2:30 pm &ndash; 3:30 pm, Dragonfly Theater</li>
</ul>
<p class="rteindent1" dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-026749fd-493a-ddd3-788d-b19d0df9198c">With each new champion, Riot Games continuously strives to level up its quality bar. But what about older champions? Enter Riot&rsquo;s Champion Relaunch team. Dedicated to quality and consistency, the team reforges classic <em>League of Legends</em> champions with new designs. Join the Relaunch team as we discuss the philosophy and technical approach behind the delicate task of reimagining older champions.</span></p>
<ul dir="ltr">
	<li>
		<span id="docs-internal-guid-026749fd-493a-ddd3-788d-b19d0df9198c"><a href="http://east.paxsite.com/schedule/panel/inside-look-at-game-design-for-league-of-legends" target="_blank">Inside Look at Game Design for <em>League of Legends</em></a></span> &ndash; Sunday, April 13 from 2:30 pm &ndash; 3:30 pm, Dragonfly Theater</li>
</ul>
<p class="rteindent1" dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-026749fd-493a-ddd3-788d-b19d0df9198c">Creating and maintaining competitive gameplay in <em>League of Legends</em> requires consistent attention to things like champion design, healthy gameplay, and building effective design communication with players. Join designers from Riot Games as we discuss how to create an engaging and satisfying gameplay experience in <em>League of Legends</em>.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
