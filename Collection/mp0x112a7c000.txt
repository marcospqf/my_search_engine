    <div class="field field-name-field-main-image field-type-image field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><img typeof="foaf:Image" src="http://www.riotgames.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_image/public/content/article/images/main/headerimage1.png?itok=i7Fe6nU3" width="700" height="300" alt="Riot Games League of Legends 2014 World Championships" /></div></div></div><div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"> <p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-05c433af-528c-6a28-5491-64fb7df7af17">It&rsquo;s been one week since our final World Championship showdown at Sangam. As the dust settles, we want to take a pause and thank players and fans all over the world who joined us on this epic journey from playoffs to Finals, and, of course, congratulate our new World Champions, Samsung White!</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-05c433af-528c-6a28-5491-64fb7df7af17">Whether you tuned in online, stayed up late to root for your favorite team, or helped us raise the roof live at the World Cup Stadium in Seoul, we owe every step of Worlds to your passion and support. You waited in insane lines, disrupted your sleep schedule, and even - in some cases - &nbsp;</span><a href="https://twitter.com/BridgetDavidson/status/513901967085559809" target="_blank">braved a typhoon</a> - to cheer along with us. We hope you enjoyed every minute of it as much as we did.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-05c433af-528c-6a28-5491-64fb7df7af17">We&rsquo;ve gotten a lot of feedback and learned a lot of lessons, so over the next few weeks we&rsquo;ll put those lessons learned into action for the 2015 season kickoff in January.</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-05c433af-528c-6a28-5491-64fb7df7af17">Many of you have also asked just how many players tuned in worldwide for the final: this will likely take us weeks to tally as we doublecheck numbers across online and TV audiences. Some folks are guessing at the numbers based on limited info, but calculating complete numbers for an international event with viewers all over the world is pretty intricate. We want to make sure we&#39;re completely confident before we give an official figure.</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-05c433af-528c-6a28-5491-64fb7df7af17">Thanks again for an awesome season, and see you in 2015!</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>- Dustin &quot;Redbeard&quot; Beck</span></p>
