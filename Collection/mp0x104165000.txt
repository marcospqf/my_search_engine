<h4>
		<strong>Latest News: DAA Announces Cross-Device Guidance Enforcement to Begin February 1, 2017</strong></h4>
<div id="ooImgDiv">
<p class="rteright"><a href="/choices/" id="ooImg"><img alt="Click to view the Consumer Opt Out Page" src="/resource/image/choice_button.gif" /></a></p>
<div>
			Make choices with participating companies under the DAA Principles</div>
</div>
<p>The Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) has announced that enforcement of its Principles for Transprancy and Consumer Control in cross-device environments will begin on February 1, 2017. The Council of Better Business Bureaus (CBBB) and Direct Marketing Association (DMA) will extend their ongoing independent oversight of the DAA Principles to cross-device tranparency and control responsibilities for first and third parties.&nbsp; <a href="http://www.aboutads.info/digital-advertising-alliance-announces-enforcement-cross-device-guidance-begin-february-1-2017">Read the press release here</a>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<hr />
<p>The Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) establishes and enforces responsible privacy practices across industry for relevant digital advertising, providing consumers with enhanced transparency and control through multifaceted&nbsp;<a href="http://www.aboutads.info/principles">Principles</a>&nbsp;that apply to Multi-Site Data and Cross-App Data gathered in either desktop or mobile environments. The DAA is an independent non-profit&nbsp;organization led by&nbsp;<a href="http://www.aboutads.info/associations">leading advertising and marketing trade associations</a>.</p>
<p><strong><span style="font-size: 16px;">For Consumers</span></strong></p>
<p><strong>Learn about Interest-Based Advertising (&quot;IBA&quot;):</strong>&nbsp;<a href="/consumers/">Find out more</a> about interest-based advertising and how it helps provide you with more relevant advertising on the websites you visit or applications you use. You&#39;ll learn how IBA&nbsp;supports the free content, products and services you use online, and what choices are provided to you through the DAA Program.</p>
<p><strong>Exercise Your Choice:</strong>&nbsp;<a href="/choices/">Visit</a> the DAA&rsquo;s Consumer Choice page, which allows users to conveniently opt out from Choice Page participating companies&rsquo; collection of Web viewing data over time and across unaffiliated sites for IBA and other applicable uses under the Principles.</p>
<p><strong>Report a Complaint:&nbsp;</strong>If you believe that you have witnessed a practice or ad may violate the Principles, you can&nbsp;<a href="/enforcement">report the incident</a>&nbsp;to either Better Business Bureaus (CBBB) or The Direct Marketing Association (DMA). Complaints may be filed by consumers, business entities or other stakeholders.</p>
<div class="break">
		&nbsp;</div>
<p>	<strong><span style="font-size: 16px;">For Interested Companies</span></strong></p>
<p><strong>Learn about the DAA Program:</strong> You can find out more about the DAA&nbsp;<a href="/principles/">Principles</a>, and our participating <a href="/associations/">Associations</a>. To learn how your company can implement the Principles, please review the <a href="http://www.aboutads.info/resources">Implementation Resources</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Register to USE the DAA Icon:</strong> Companies participating in Program may use the DAA Icon as a means for providing enhanced notice of interest-based advertising practices. If you would like to register to use the icon, please <a href="/participants/icon/">click here</a>.&nbsp;here. To separately register your company participate in the DAA&rsquo;s AppChoices tool, please <a href="https://www.aboutads.info/appchoices-inquiry-form-sf">click here</a>.</p>
<p>	<strong>Report a Complaint:&nbsp;</strong>If you believe that you have witnessed a practice or ad that may violate the Principles, you can&nbsp;<a href="/enforcement">report the incident</a>&nbsp;to incident to either Better Business Bureaus (CBBB) or The Direct Marketing Association (DMA). Complaints may be filed by consumers, business entities or other stakeholders.</p>
<div class="break">
		&nbsp;</div>
<p><strong><span style="font-size: 16px;">For Companies Engaged in Online Behavioral Advertising</span></strong></p>
<p>To enhance the transparency of interest-based advertising, the DAA encourages companies qualifying as third parties under the Principles to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
			<strong>Inform consumers</strong> about their data practices through clear, meaningful and prominent notices.</li>
<li>
			<strong>Display the DAA&nbsp;<a href="/participants/icon/">Icon</a></strong> so Icon so that consumers find out about interest-based advertising, learn about the data practices associated with advertisements they receive, and opt-out if they choose.</li>
<li>
			<strong><a href="/participants/register/">Register</a></strong> to receive information about how to be listed on the <strong>Consumer Choice Page</strong>, where consumers will be able to easily opt out from data collection by participating companies for applicable practices</li>
</ul>
