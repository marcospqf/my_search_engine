<div id="faq-a-72" class="faq-answer"> <p>Visit our&nbsp;<a href="careers" target="_blank">Careers</a>&nbsp;section, which&nbsp;includes information about what it&#39;s like to be a Rioter, how to apply for roles and what sorts of opportunities are available. If you&#39;re looking for career advice,&nbsp;try hitting up our <a href="http://www.linkedin.com/groups/Inside-Riot-Games-People-Game-3544410?gid=3544410">LinkedIn group</a>. LinkedIn is a site for professionally focused discussion and some seekers have received good advice when they ask for help there. Please do try some internet searches first, though. There&#39;s lots of great advice already published online about how to break into the games industry, and there are a number of online resources that will put you on the right path. We hire self-starters, so that means doing some good ol&#39; fashioned Googling before you hit us up with questions.</p>

<p>Can&#39;t make it down to LA, but still want a look? Check out this behind-the-scenes look of our&nbsp;<a href="http://www.riotgames.com/articles/20150420/1663/twas-night-our-move-la" target="_blank">new office</a>!</p>

<div id="faq-a-2357" class="faq-answer"> <p>Want to create an app, website, t-shirt or something else with our intellectual property, but don&rsquo;t know what&rsquo;s legally kosher? &nbsp;Check out our <a href="http://www.riotgames.com/legal-jibber-jabber">Legal Jibber Jabber</a>.  </div>  </li>
          <li class="views-row views-row-2 views-row-even faq-group">  
          <a href="#" title="Got an awesome idea for League of Legends? " rel="faq-a-495" class="faq-question">Got an awesome idea for League of Legends? </a>
<div id="faq-a-495" class="faq-answer"> <p>If you&#39;ve got an awesome idea for a skin, champion or how to improve <em>League of Legends</em>, the best place for these is <a href="http://boards.na.leagueoflegends.com/" target="_blank">http://boards.na.leagueoflegends.com/</a>.&nbsp;We do read the forums and pay attention to the ideas that players share with us there.&nbsp;</p>
<p>For a bunch of reasons, we can&#39;t accept submissions for skin or champion concepts, but we&#39;ve noticed that many summoners love coming up with concepts so we created <a href="http://boards.na.leagueoflegends.com/en/c/skin-champion-concepts" target="_blank"><span class="s1">this forum</span></a> where you can post and share these ideas. If you post here, you&#39;ll get others&#39; feedback on your concept.</p>
<p>Please note that this forum&#39;s purpose isn&#39;t to help Riot find the newest champion of the League. Rather, we want to give you a platform to express your creativity.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
